I am a fourth year PhD student advised by Prof. A. Erdem Sarıyüce in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, University at Buffalo. My research focues on graph mining, temporal networks, and dense subgraph discovery. Currently I am working on the temporal motifs and core & truss decompostion.

I got my M.S in Geographic Information Science from University at Buffalo, under the supervision of Prof. Ling Bian. I received B.S. in GIS from Wuhan University, China.
